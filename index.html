Privacy Policy

Qibla Direction · Mekka Finder

Short version: We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. Everything the app needs is computed locally on your device. There are no servers, no accounts, no analytics, and no advertising.

What we do not collect

Qibla Direction · Mekka Finder operates entirely on your device. The app does not send your location, identity, prayer history, settings, or any other information to us or to any third party. We have no servers, no databases, and no way to identify you.

Information used on your device

With your permission, the app reads the following information solely to provide its core features. None of this data leaves your iPhone.

  • Location. Used to calculate the direction to the Kaaba and to compute prayer times for your area. Location is requested only when the app is in use and is processed locally.
  • Compass / device orientation. Used to display your live heading on the Qibla compass. Read directly from the device sensors at runtime.
  • Notifications. If you enable Adhan reminders, the app schedules local notifications on your device. They are not sent through any server. You can disable them in iOS Settings or within the app at any time.

Third-party services

The app does not embed analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. The Qur'an verses, Adhan audio, prayer-time calculation library, and typeface are bundled inside the app itself and require no network connection to function.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages. Because we collect no personal information from anyone, we collect none from children either.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and adjust the effective date below. Material changes will also be reflected in future App Store release notes.
